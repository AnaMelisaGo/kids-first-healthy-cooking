KIDS first healthy cooking class is fun, free, and friendly community. We have 3 classes a week to choose from and different timetables to alter meals and recipes. When you sign up you can choose one or more classes.


Days before the class starts, we will send you an email with the link to access the class via Google Meet and the menu of what we are going to cook that day including the lists of ingredients to purchase beforehand. We keep all the ingredients healthy, simple and affordable for everyone's pocket. And of course we make sure it’s delicious and fun!
